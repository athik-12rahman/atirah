About

Atirah Consulting Solutions Ltd is a UK-based independent consultancy. We partner with social housing providers, local government and landlords to solve operational problems fast and leave teams stronger than we found them.

Typical outcomes: lower arrears, fewer escalations, faster turnarounds, cleaner audits, and happier residents.

How we work

  • Listen first — understand your current process and constraints.
  • Co-design — shape solutions with the people who run the work.
  • Deliver — timeboxed sprints with clear owners and measures.
  • Hand over — documents, training and dashboards that last.
